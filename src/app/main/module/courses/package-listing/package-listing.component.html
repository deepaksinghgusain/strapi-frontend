What is your stress costing you?
Money? Clients? Health?

Live event

It’s time to retrain your mind, and take control of how you respond to stress so you can reach your highest potential and give your clients the best of you. We look forward to seeing you in our workshop.

As a finance professional, you wear multiple hats when dealing with clients. And learning how to manage your stress is a crucial component to success. Did you know that EAs, CPAs, and finance professionals face significantly more stress than the average professional, as per Financial Planning magazine?

According to Mental Health America, stress costs US employers an estimated$500 billion in lost productivity annually. Poorly managed stress, time, and resources can become detrimental to your career. You may experience, lower productivity, failure to achieve goals, burnout, anxiety, depression, and poor physical health in the face of market volatility, increased competition, shrinking margins, limited time to see all your clients, meeting your targets and your client’s expectations. We know stress is an inevitable part of the job, but it doesn’t have to be costly. There are tools in this workshop and actionable steps you can take that we believe will positively impact the outcome of your coming season.

In our “Stress Less & Manage Better” Workshop, you will:

  • Learn to become more resilient, invoking your natural capacity at addressing challenges affecting your behavior, relationships, body, peace of mind and bottom line
  • Acquire a skill set to help you manage your stress levels and avoid burning out during the most chaotic time of the year
  • Explore how stress affects your body and how you could create balance and harmony with tools you already have
  • Leave this workshop confident and equipped with techniques you can apply to cultivate a more balanced mental health.
